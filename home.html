INDIAN ARMED FORCES

Indian Army

The Indian Army is the land-based branch of the Indian Armed Forces. Established in 1895, it is one of the largest standing armies in the world with over 1.4 million active personnel.

Indian Navy

The Indian Navy is the naval branch of the Indian Armed Forces. With 67,252 personnel and 150 ships and submarines, it safeguards maritime borders and projects India's naval power across the Indo-Pacific region.

Indian Air Force

The Indian Air Force is the air arm of the Indian Armed Forces. With approximately 170,000 personnel and 2,123 aircraft, it is the fourth largest air force in the world and maintains strategic air superiority.
